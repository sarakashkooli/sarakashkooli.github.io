About Me

I am a clinical nutritionist and dedicated researcher with over 4 years of experience in evidence synthesis. My work focuses on the intersection of metabolic health and nutraceuticals, specifically managing chronic conditions such as Type 2 Diabetes, NAFLD, and obesity. With a track record of 14 peer-reviewed ISI publications, I specialize in transforming complex clinical data into actionable health insights. My mission is to bridge the gap between clinical dietetics and rigorous epidemiological research.
