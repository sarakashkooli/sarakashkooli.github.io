About Me

I am a clinical nutritionist and dedicated researcher with over 4 years of experience in evidence synthesis. My work focuses on the intersection of metabolic health and nutraceuticals, specifically managing chronic conditions such as Type 2 Diabetes, NAFLD, and obesity. With a strong foundation in clinical dietetics and a passion for rigorous scientific inquiry, I specialize in transforming complex clinical data into actionable health insights through systematic reviews and meta-analyses. My mission is to bridge the gap between clinical practice and cutting-edge epidemiological research, contributing to evidence-based advancements in nutritional science.
