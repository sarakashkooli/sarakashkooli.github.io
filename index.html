About me

I am a nutritionist and dietitian focused on preventing and managing chronic diseases like diabetes, NAFLD, and cognitive disorders. My work highlights how nutrients such as omega-3s, probiotics, and vitamins support brain and body health. I am a passionate nutritionist and researcher with 4+ years of experience in clinical dietetics. With 14 published ISI articles and 5 years of hospital experience, I create personalized diet plans for patients. Through my private practice, I now help people use nutrition to prevent diseases and improve well-being. I aim to turn scientific knowledge into practical, everyday solutions for better health.
