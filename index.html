Data Science

Automating Unit Standardization in Meta-Analyses

Biochemical markers across trials often use highly inconsistent metrics. Learn how Python pipeline automations eliminate manual translation errors...

During my work across 14 systematic meta-analyses, manual unit normalization was a major bottleneck. I constructed a lightweight python script to process and unify reporting metrics dynamically:

def convert_units(val, current_unit, analyte):
    if analyte == "glucose" and current_unit == "mg/dL":
        return val * 0.0555  # converts to mmol/L
    return val

Automating this flow reduces statistical processing errors, securing cleaner dataset entries for comprehensive meta-regressions.

Epidemiology

The Imperative of Long-term Cohorts in Healthy Ageing

Clinical trials provide isolated insights, but to understand the trajectory of healthy aging, nutritional epidemiology must rely on prospective, large-scale cohorts...

To tackle chronic metabolic conditions, we must move beyond short-term interventions. Cohorts allow us to study the interactions of lifestyle and biology across decades. Understanding diet as a longitudinal exposure is key to shifting public policy from reactive disease management to proactive prevention.

Nutrigenetics

Bridging the Gap Between Diet-Gene Interactions and Public Health

Are general dietary guidelines truly effective? This post reviews how polymorphisms influence individuals' metabolic responses...

Nutrigenetics represents the frontier of modern preventive medicine. My early literature work on FTO polymorphism showed clear modulations in Leptin levels. Integrating genomics into large epidemiology frameworks will define the next generation of precise clinical diets.
