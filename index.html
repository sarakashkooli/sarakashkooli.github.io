Research Interests

Studying nutritional needs of athletes in different sports and the effect of dietary supplements on sports performance. Developing nutritional protocols for improved recovery and performance.

Plant-Based Nutrition

Investigating the impact of vegetarian diets on general health and disease management. Developing practical solutions to meet nutritional needs in plant-based diets.

Research on the connection between diet and mental health. Examining the effect of specific nutrients on cognitive function and mood disorders such as depression and anxiety.
